About Me

I am an undergraduate student from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Under the guidance of Professor Xinggang Wang from the School of Telecommunications, I will collaborate with Huawei to start my PhD career this September. My research interests mainly focus on computer vision, particularly on accelerating and applying 3D reconstruction (NeRF).

Additionally, I have previously interned at the User Growth Team of ByteDance, mainly working on the development of GPH (Generalized Propensity Hazard Model) and Causal Forest Population Model. I also participated in the research and development of automatic driving perception models at SenseTime.
